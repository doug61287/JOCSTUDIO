Pro Tips & Common Pitfalls

JOCstudio

How to Read HHC Schedules Effectively

1
Start with the Division Summary
Before diving into line items, review the Division 21 summary page. It shows all major categories and helps you understand the schedule structure.
2
Match Specifications Exactly
HHC line items are specific. "Schedule 40" is different from "Schedule 10." "1/2" NPT" is different from "1/2" grooved." Get the specs right.
3
Check for Bundled vs. Separate Items
Some line items include labor + material, others are material-only with separate installation. Read the full description carefully.
4
Use the Index & Search
The UPB PDF is searchable. Use Ctrl+F to find items quickly. Search by CSI number (21 10 XX) or keyword.

Common Pitfalls to Avoid

!
Wrong Line Item Selection
Using "pipe replacement" when you should use "new installation" â€” they're priced differently.
!
Missing Support Items
Forgot hangers, firestops, or drain/fill valves? They add up fast.
!
Underestimating Fittings
Rule of thumb: fittings = 15-25% of pipe cost. Don't forget elbows, tees, and couplings.
!
Ignoring Existing Conditions
Working in occupied hospital? Add time for infection control, off-hours work, coordination.
!
Skipping System Drain/Recharge
Every modification requires drain down and recharge. Don't forget these line items.
!
No Mobilization
Even small jobs need mob/demob. Include it in every proposal.

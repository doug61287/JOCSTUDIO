## Hero Metrics (How We Measure Success)

### Leading Indicators
- Time to first win (target: <14 days)
- Community participation rate (target: 30%)
- NPS score (target: 50+)
- Feature adoption rate (target: 70%)

### Lagging Indicators
- Contracts won per hero (target: 5/month)
- Hours saved per hero (target: 20/month)
- Referral rate (target: 20%)
- Upgrade rate (target: 15%)

### Hero Health Score
Composite metric of:
- Login frequency (30%)
- Projects completed (30%)
- Community participation (20%)
- Support tickets (10%)
- Feature usage (10%)

**Score:** 0-100
- 80-100: Hero (proactive outreach for case studies)
- 60-79: Rising (nurture with education)
- 40-59: At-risk (intervention needed)
- 0-39: Churn risk (urgent outreach)

## Hero Support Playbook

### When a Hero is Struggling

**Detect:**
- No login in 7 days
- Support ticket with frustration
- Failed project/loss mentioned
- Community silence

**Respond (within 2 hours):**
1. Personal message from founder
2. Offer 1:1 call
3. Ask: "What's blocking you from winning?"
4. Provide specific help (template, training, etc.)

**Follow-up:**
- Check in 3 days later
- Celebrate small wins
- Escalate to success team if needed

---

### When a Hero Succeeds

**Detect:**
- Won contract posted
- Time saved mentioned
- Positive social media post
- Referral made

**Respond (within 24 hours):**
1. Public celebration (Discord, LinkedIn)
2. Personal thank you video
3. Ask for case study interview
4. Invite to refer others (with commission)
